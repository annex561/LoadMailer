## 2026-06-07 — Lease-to-Own Owner-Operator Program
- Model: lease-to-own on company-owned trucks, driver runs under the company's seasoned MC authority. Moat = eliminating the 6-12mo new-authority "death valley" (brokers won't load new MCs; new owner-ops starve in the ramp and default from no revenue, not from the truck payment).
- EQUIPMENT: 26ft box trucks WITH LIFTGATE (non-CDL capable, ≤26,000 GVWR). Implications: cheaper trucks ($25-40k used) fit the $5k/$800/36mo envelope with room; cheaper insurance; smaller down; NON-CDL = much larger driver pool; lower regulatory profile than CDL lease-purchase mills. Still needs MC for interstate for-hire. Liftgate → optimized for no-dock final-mile (residential/small-retail/job-site) + LTL. Freight: final-mile big-and-bulky + Amazon Relay as floor; expedite hot-parts out of the SE auto corridor (Nissan Smyrna / GM Spring Hill / VW Chattanooga) as upside. NOTE: docs/shipper-target-list.md is 53' TL freight — for FUTURE tractor scale, not box trucks.
- Down payment: $5,000 truck (equity, non-refundable, forfeit on default) + $1,500 first-month insurance (= one month of actual quote). No credit check (secured by holding title + controlling cash flow).
- Term: 36 months at $800/mo (~$185/wk). Trucks priced ~$25-30k so $5k down + $800×36 ≈ $33,800 pays off exactly at term. Early buyout allowed at remaining balance.
- Fee: 20% of gross on dispatched loads = dispatch + authority. Does NOT cover fuel/factoring/insurance (driver pays those).
- Cash flow: company factors all loads (money hits company first). Weekly settlement + itemized statement. Deduction waterfall: 20% → insurance → truck payment → maintenance escrow ($75/wk) → factoring fee → remainder to driver.
- Insurance: truck on company's commercial policy (required under company authority). Company provides liability+cargo; driver pays physical damage + flat margin + own deductibles. Safety-out clause (bad MVR/claims = removal).
- Default: performance-based, not "missed payment" (auto-deduct makes missing impossible). Triggers: 7-day idle truck, 3-week arrears ceiling, abandonment, abuse/neglect. Consequence: forfeit down payment + equity, truck returns.
- Coupling: package held together by ASSET OWNERSHIP (company truck on company insurance/authority), NOT forced-dispatch language (avoids misclassification + predatory framing). Driver may decline loads / take approved home time. Title transfers + full release at payoff.
- Rule: FREIGHT LEADS TRUCKS 1:1. Never seat a driver ahead of committed freight. Freight stack = floor (Amazon Relay [signed up], government via SAM.gov, dedicated lanes/Saia) + spot upside.
- Legal budget: $0-660. OOIDA free review (816-229-5791) first, then attorney finalize. Separate LLC for the leasing arm.
- Caution: regulatory heat rising (FMCSA Truck Leasing Task Force Jan-2025 called lease-purchase "irredeemable tools of fraud"; Predatory Truck Leasing Act pending). Run the clean/honest version as both compliance shield and marketing wedge. Do NOT multiply/season multiple MCs (chameleon-carrier flag) — grow freight under the one authority.
