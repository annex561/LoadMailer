# Box-Truck Freight Sources — Master List
**Carrier:** dock-high 26ft box, tuck-under liftgate, non-CDL, own (seasoned) MC/DOT authority. Lease-to-own owner-op program. Footprint: TN/KY/GA/AL/MS/AR/MO + southern IL/IN/OH; hubs Memphis–Nashville–Atlanta; occasional FL/Carolinas. No Northeast, no West.

## Strategic findings (read first)
1. **Big retailers/OEMs don't hire one-truck carriers.** They hire final-mile 3PLs, and those 3PLs onboard MC-authority box-truck owner-ops. The 3PL on-ramps are your fastest revenue.
2. **Big LTL "city P&D subcontracting" is a myth.** Saia, ODFL, SEFL, AAA Cooper, R+L, ABF staff city P&D with W-2 employees. They only sub linehaul (Class A) and brokered final-mile. Don't waste time chasing LTL P&D.
3. **Dock-high is your edge** — it opens middle-mile facility transfers, drayage/transload, and dock-to-dock that liftgate-only residential carriers can't touch. The tuck-under liftgate ALSO wins jobsite/no-dock drops. You play both sides.
4. **Your seasoned authority clears the gates instantly.** Amazon Relay needs authority active ≥180 days; several programs want 1yr. Your drivers run under YOUR aged authority, so they pass on day one. The moat pays off here.
5. **Insurance bar to build once:** $1M auto liability + $100K cargo + workers' comp (+ $1M general liability for any in-home work). Build the COI to that spec and you clear nearly every program below in one underwriting pass.

| Cargo | $100,000 (some brokers $250K) |
| General liability | $1,000,000 (any in-home / white-glove) |
| Workers' comp | Per state; required by Amazon + most 3PLs |
| ELD | Required (>10,001 lbs) |
| Truck age | Generally 2015+ |
| Team | 2-person for white-glove furniture; 1-person OK for middle-mile, auto parts, building materials, medical, packaging |

## D. Matching drivers to freight (model note)
- **Solo lease-to-own drivers** → middle-mile (Amazon Relay, dedicated DC-to-store), auto-parts routes, building-materials jobsite (1-person liftgate), medical, packaging routes. These don't need a helper.
- **White-glove furniture** (RXO/Ryder/Diakon) needs a 2-person team — either build the helper cost in or steer solo drivers to the 1-person freight above.
